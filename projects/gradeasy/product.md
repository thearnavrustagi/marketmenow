# Gradeasy

**Website:** [gradeasy.ai](https://gradeasy.ai)

## What is Gradeasy?

Gradeasy is an AI-powered grading assistant built for K-12 teachers. Upload any assignment — handwritten, typed, PDF, or photo — and Gradeasy grades it against your rubric in seconds. Teachers keep full control over final grades; the AI handles the heavy lifting.

## Key Features

- **Instant AI Grading** — Grades assignments against rubrics in seconds, not hours
- **Supports All Formats** — Works with images, PDFs, and handwritten student work
- **Custom Rubrics** — Create your own rubrics or let the AI generate one from your assignment
- **Detailed Feedback** — Provides per-criterion feedback so students know exactly where to improve
- **Teacher Control** — Teachers always have the final say on grades; override any AI decision
- **Time Savings** — Saves teachers 8-15 hours per week on grading
- **Free to Try** — No credit card required to get started

## Who It's For

K-12 teachers who are overwhelmed by grading workload — spending evenings and weekends grading, struggling with inconsistent feedback, or burning out from repetitive assessment tasks.
